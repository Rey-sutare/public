Welcome to Batangas City

Nestled in the heart of the Philippines, Batangas City is a paradise where nature's most spectacular creations come together. From the iconic Taal Volcano to the world-renowned marine biodiversity of Verde Island Passage, every corner tells a story of natural wonder.

Whether you're seeking adventure on mountain trails, tranquility beneath the waves, or the simple pleasure of watching the sun set over pristine beaches, Batangas City offers an unforgettable escape into nature's embrace.

Visitor Guidelines

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Dispose of trash properly - keep nature clean
  • Follow designated trails and marked paths
  • Do not disturb wildlife or remove natural specimens
  • Swimming only in designated safe areas
  • Hire licensed tour guides for safety
  • Check weather conditions before outdoor activities
  • Bring adequate sun protection and water
  • Support local businesses and communities
  • Photography for commercial use requires permits
